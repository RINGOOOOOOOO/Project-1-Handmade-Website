I, like a fish, freely roam in the vast ocean.

Only to realize, I am also a little fish confined in a massive fish tank.

No matter how hard I struggle, I cannot swim beyond this transparent boundary.

I see a fish, a fish emitting a green glow, floating in the air.

She sways her tail, still wearing a smile.

Her smile suddenly fills me with sadness.

I cannot possess this fish.

Faithful as a dog, tender as a cat,

deeply loving like a cherished soulmate.

Passing by an old bridge, memories of a song from my youth come to mind.

Unconsciously, I softly hum it in the car.

I escort a fish home.

Back to the true home.

She waves her tail, sliding into the vast blue ocean.

This time, I really fall asleep.

I tenderly kiss my fish.
